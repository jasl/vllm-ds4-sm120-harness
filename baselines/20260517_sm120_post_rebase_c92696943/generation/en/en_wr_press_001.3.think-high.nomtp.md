Each native plant you add is a stepping stone for a pollinator on the move.”

The trail begins at the eastern entrance of the meadow loop and winds gently through a series of themed stations. To ensure the trail is fully accessible to all learners, Harborleaf installed **tactile signage** along the entire route. Admission to the Pollinator Path is included with regular garden entry; Harborleaf members and children under five enter free.
