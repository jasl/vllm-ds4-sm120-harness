### Key Terms (with Definitions)
1. **Water cycle**: The continuous movement and storage of water on Earth, driven by solar energy and gravity.
2. **Evaporation**: Process by which solar energy turns liquid water into water vapor.
3. **Evapotranspiration**: The main pathway for water to move from land and ocean surfaces into the atmosphere.
4. **Precipitation**: Rain, snow, or hail that falls from clouds due to gravity.
5. **Runoff**: Water that flows across the land surface toward streams or oceans.
6. **Infiltration**: Movement of water from the surface into the ground.
7. **Groundwater recharge**: Process by which infiltrated water replenishes underground aquifers.
8. **Aquifer**: Underground layer that stores and transmits groundwater.
