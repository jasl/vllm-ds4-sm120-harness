2. **Evaporation**: The process where liquid water changes into water vapor due to energy from the sun.
3. **Evapotranspiration**: The main way water moves from land and oceans into the atmosphere, combining evaporation and plant transpiration.
4. **Precipitation**: Rain, snow, or hail that falls from clouds to the Earth’s surface, driven by gravity.

6. **Infiltration**: The movement of water from the land surface into the ground.
7. **Groundwater recharge**: The process by which infiltrated water enters underground aquifers.
8. **Natural discharge**: The return of groundwater to the surface through rivers, oceans, or springs.
